Cookie Policy

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. We use minimal storage to ensure our website functions correctly.

What we use

Kuro-Flow does not use traditional cookies for tracking or advertising. We use sessionStorage — a browser-based temporary storage that is cleared when you close your browser tab. This is used exclusively to:

Storage keyPurposeDuration
kf_keyRemember your login state in the Members AreaSession only
kf_analysis_*Cache the weekly market analysis to avoid reloadingSession only
exit_popup_shownPrevent the exit popup from showing multiple timesSession only

Third-party services

Our website uses the following third-party services that may set their own cookies or storage:

  • Google Fonts — for typography (no tracking)
  • Gumroad — for payment processing (their own cookie policy applies)
  • Flagcdn.com — for country flag images in notifications
  • TwelveData — for live USDJPY price data

Your choices

Since we do not use tracking cookies, there is nothing to opt out of. You can clear your browser's sessionStorage at any time through your browser settings without affecting your experience on other websites.
